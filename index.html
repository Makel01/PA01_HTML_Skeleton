Why We Gather

BlackCS is a student-led space where Black technologists at FAMU swap skills, celebrate wins, and build projects that uplift our community.

We host weekly meetups, peer tutoring nights, and mentor chats with alumni so newcomers feel welcome and supported from day one.

Ways to Get Involved

  • Join project nights to pair-program and ship small web tools.
  • Sign up for peer-led study circles before midterms and finals.
  • Volunteer at community workshops introducing local teens to coding.
